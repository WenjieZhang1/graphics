User Instructions:
1.Use Up/Down/Right/Left Arrows to move up/down/right/left
2.Use W and S to look up and down
3.Use R/F/T/G/Y/H to change color
4.Use Z to switch Phong Shading and Gouraud Shading
5.Use U/J/I/K/O/L to move light
6.Use Space to swith Bling-Phong Lighting and Phong Lighting
